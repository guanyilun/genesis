Genomic L→M vs. Executed LOAD (Normal condition)

What this means: The genomic LOAD→MOVE adjacency (blue) measures whether organisms carry the stigmergic circuit in their genome. The executed LOAD frequency (orange) measures whether they actually use it. The two decouple at tick 50k — the circuit persists genetically while falling out of the execution profile. This is selection maintaining the potential for stigmergy without the cost of executing it. Think of it as a "vestigial trait" that could re-activate if conditions change.

Hypothesis for future experiments: If we took organisms from tick 150k (genomic L→M intact, execution zero) and transplanted them into a naive world with high trace information, would they re-activate LOAD execution faster than a control? This would test whether the genome retains "latent" stigmergic capability.
